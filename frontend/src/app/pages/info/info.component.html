Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal personal information necessary to process your orders: your name, email address, and shipping details. Passwords are securely hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for order processing, account management, and customer support. We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in an encrypted MySQL database. Communication between the frontend and backend is secured via HTTPS. Authentication tokens (JWT) are used for session management.

4. Cookies

We use only essential browser storage (localStorage) for authentication tokens and shopping cart contents. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at hello@jamaicanheritage.com.

6. Consent

By creating an account and using our services, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.
